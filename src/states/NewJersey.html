Register at Home or School

Students have two options about where to register to vote.

Students may choose to register at their campus address or at their permanent or home address. You may only be registered and vote in one location.

What type of ID do I need?

To Register

New Jersey’s voter registration form asks for your New Jersey driver’s license or ID number, or the last four digits of your Social Security number.

To Vote

Most New Jersey voters do not need to show ID at the polls in order to vote.

However, if you are a first-time voter who registered to vote by mail without providing either your New Jersey driver’s license number, non-driver ID number, or the last four digits of your Social Security number, you will need to show valid ID before voting. Acceptable forms of ID include:

  • A New Jersey driver's license or other state-issued ID
  • A U.S. Passport
  • A military or other government ID
  • A student or job ID
  • A store membership card (like a Costco card)
  • A bank statement, paycheck, car registration, rent receipt, sample ballot, government document, or utility bill

How can I vote?

By Mail

New Jersey voters who wish to vote by mail must submit an application. You do not need to provide a reason. The county clerk must receive applications for vote by mail ballots seven days before Election Day. A voter may also apply in person to the County Clerk until 3:00 p.m. the day before the election.

The ballot must be postmarked no later than the day of the election, and the County Board of Election must receive it no later than 48 hours after 8:00 p.m. the day of the election.

Early In-Person

You may also request a vote by mail ballot in-person at the County election office. If you make the request in person, you have until 3 p.m. the day before Election Day. If you return a vote by mail ballot in person, you must provide a proof of identification.

On Election day

Voting sites will be open from 6 a.m. to 8 p.m. on Election Day. If you are in line by the closing time then you must be allowed to vote. You can find information about your voting site and hours here.
